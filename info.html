About Me.

My name is Nathaniel Sean Jaworski and I will soon be majoring in Computer Science at The University of Missouri, Columbia. I have a strong passion for learning and discovering new things. My connection with computer science started as I saw the potential that was growing in the field and wanted to put myself on route where I could potentially be apart of the future. My strive to make a change and discover the undiscovered has given my many passions throughout my life, such as interests in astrophysics, all types of math, forensics, the oceans, nature, and many others.
